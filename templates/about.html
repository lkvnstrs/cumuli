    Instructions

    Show your favorite artist by going to /u/soundcloudname.
    They're represented by that lonely, orange bubble.

    Add more names to the URL to see some blue bubbles representing the artists they all follow, such as /u/user1+user2
    Click the bubbles to see who's who.

    The more artists you add,
    the more interesting the diagrams get. A bigger blue bubble represents an artist more of your artists follow.
